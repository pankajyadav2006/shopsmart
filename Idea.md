# Project Idea: ReStock – Smart Subscription & Reorder E-Commerce

## Overview
ReStock is a smart e-commerce platform focused on **frequently used daily products** such as groceries, toiletries, stationery, and household essentials.  
Instead of one-time shopping, ReStock helps users **track usage patterns and reorder items easily**, making routine shopping faster and hassle-free.

---

## Problem Statement
- Users often forget to reorder daily-use items.
- Repetitive shopping wastes time.
- Existing platforms focus on browsing rather than convenience and automation.

---

## Proposed Solution
ReStock simplifies shopping by:
- Allowing users to reorder frequently purchased items with one click.
- Suggesting reorders based on previous purchase history.
- Offering optional subscriptions for recurring products.

---

## Key Features

### 1. Smart Reorder System (Core)
- Tracks previously purchased products.
- Suggests reorders after a certain time interval.
- One-click “Reorder Cart” option.

### 2. Subscription-Based Shopping
- Users can subscribe to items (weekly / monthly).
- Flexible pause or cancel options.
- Discount incentives for subscriptions.

### 3. Product & Cart Management
- Browse products by category.
- Add to cart and checkout.
- Order history and status tracking.

### 4. Admin Dashboard
- Add, update, and manage products.
- Manage inventory and orders.
- View basic sales insights.

---

## Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** React.js
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express.js
- **Database:** SQLite3 with Prisma ORM
- **Deployment:** Vercel (frontend), Render (backend)

---

## Unique Value Proposition
- Focuses on convenience rather than exploration.
- Reduces repetitive shopping effort.
- Clean and scalable subscription model.
- Suitable for daily-use products.

---

## Future Enhancements
- Usage prediction with analytics.
- Reminder notifications.
- Personalized discounts.
- Mobile app / PWA support.

---

## Conclusion
ReStock is a practical e-commerce platform designed for efficiency and repeat usage, offering a smarter way to manage everyday shopping needs.
